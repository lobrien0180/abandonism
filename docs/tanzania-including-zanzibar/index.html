Tanzania: Zanzibar

Where to start for Zanzibar?! A little piece of paradise, with plenty of history and architecture to explore.

Another place we also did very well with food, lots of fresh fish, fresh vegetables and fruit, oh and the spices! Do get yourself on a spice tour.

 You can spend hours exploring the laneways of Stonetown, lots of tourist shops (this is where my obsession with Tanzanite began) and quite places to sit with a drink.

Or you can head to one of the beach areas and chill out there. We headed to Nungwi and hung out there for a few days.
